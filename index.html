About me

This is me
And this is my bio
My name is Marco jansen. I was born in Germany and grew up in a town called Moenchengladbach. After I finished school, I have been in the military for 10 months. I then started an apprenticeship in electronics with a main subject of information technology. After completing the traineeship I continued to work for the company Scheid&Bachmann which specialized in automated parking systems. I received another specialized traineeship for 12 months to support the software of the product.

After 2 years in the hotline service department I was sent to the US for 3.5 years to work in Baltimore, MD. I fulfilled a roll of hotline support engineer and my responsibility shifted from just Maryland at first to later support the entire east coast. After 3.5 years I then was offered a position in Perth, Australia as a software engineer. I have been with the company for 3 years before being promoted to a senior software engineer. Currently I am participating in a web developers bootcamp at UWA and am hoping to change career into a more creative role.
